Snaplate — Terms & Conditions (Subscription Addendum)

Last updated: 2026-06-25

These terms cover Snaplate's optional purchases. They supplement Snaplate's general terms and Apple's standard EULA.

1. Free scans, subscription & credits

Every new device can analyse 5 meals for free — no account and no card required. Beyond that, Snaplate offers an optional auto-renewable monthly subscription:

PlanMonthly priceMonthly analyses
Snaplate ProShown in the App Store before purchase (varies by storefront)200 analyses

Pro subscribers can also buy an optional one-time 50-credit top-up. Top-up credits are used only after your monthly credits are spent and do not expire.

One credit is consumed per meal analysis. An analysis that fails on our side does not consume a credit. Unused monthly credits do not roll over; the monthly allowance resets each billing period.

2. How analysis works (data)

To recognise a meal, your photo is transmitted to our servers (AWS, United States) for analysis and then discarded — it is not stored and is never used to train AI models. This applies to every scan, free or paid. See the Privacy Notice. Analysis requires an internet connection.

3. Billing, renewal, and cancellation

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
  • The subscription auto-renews each month unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Manage or cancel anytime in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your device.
  • Prices may vary by storefront and are shown before purchase.

4. Restoring purchases

Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, not to a Snaplate account. Reinstalling the app or using a new device restores your subscription automatically; you can also tap Restore Purchases on the Subscription screen.

5. Refunds

Refunds are handled by Apple under their policies. Request a refund via Apple (reportaproblem.apple.com).

6. Service & changes

Analysis depends on third-party model providers and network availability and requires an internet connection. If the service is temporarily unavailable or your allowance is exhausted, analysis may be unavailable until it recovers or your allowance renews. We may change models, pricing, or allowances on a going-forward basis; material changes will be communicated in-app or on the website.

7. No medical advice

Snaplate provides nutrition estimates for informational purposes only and is not medical or dietary advice.
