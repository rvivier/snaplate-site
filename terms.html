Snaplate — Terms & Conditions (Subscription Addendum)

Last updated: 2026-06-06

These terms cover Snaplate's paid subscriptions. They supplement Snaplate's general terms and Apple's standard EULA.

1. Subscriptions

Snaplate offers optional auto-renewable monthly subscriptions that unlock more capable AI models:

TierModelMonthly priceMonthly credit allowance
AdvancedMeta Llama 4 Maverick (cloud)US$4.99 (or local equivalent)500 analyses
PremiumAnthropic Sonnet (cloud)US$9.99 (or local equivalent)250 analyses

One credit is consumed per meal analysis. An analysis that fails on our side does not consume a credit. Unused credits do not roll over; the allowance resets each billing period.

2. How paid analysis works (data)

On a paid tier, your meal photo is transmitted to our servers (AWS, United States) for analysis and then discarded — it is not stored and is never used to train AI models. See the Privacy Notice. The free tier processes photos entirely on your device.

3. Billing, renewal, and cancellation

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
  • The subscription auto-renews each month unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Manage or cancel anytime in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your device.
  • Prices may vary by storefront and are shown before purchase.

4. Restoring purchases

Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, not to a Snaplate account. Reinstalling the app or using a new device restores your subscription automatically; you can also tap Restore Purchases on the Subscription screen.

5. Refunds

Refunds are handled by Apple under their policies. Request a refund via Apple (reportaproblem.apple.com).

6. Service & changes

The paid tiers depend on third-party model providers and network availability. If a remote model is temporarily unavailable or your monthly allowance is exhausted, the app falls back to the on-device model so you can keep analysing meals. We may change models, pricing, or allowances on a going-forward basis; material changes will be communicated in-app or on the website.

7. No medical advice

Snaplate provides nutrition estimates for informational purposes only and is not medical or dietary advice.
